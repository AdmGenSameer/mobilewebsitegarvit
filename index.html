Season 4.0

This season has been incredibly rough for our team, marred by a series of accidents and scams that tested our resilience. Despite the challenges, we refused to be deterred and pushed forward. Our unwavering determination led us to overcome these obstacles and emerge stronger than ever. Throughout it all, we persevered, and through hard work and dedication, we managed to design and build a remarkable new ATV. This exceptional vehicle now stands ready to compete in the upcoming Mega ATV championship. The struggles we faced have only served to reinforce our team spirit and commitment to success. We are more motivated than ever to seize victory in the face of adversity.

Team GarVIT

Team GarVIT the official E-Baja team of VIT Bhopal University was founded in 2018 . The team innovate and collaborate with bright minds to push our limits and go beyond of what is possible. Every year team showcases their engineering prowess and commitment to sustainability by building an Electric ATV from scratch and competing against various other teams to achieve the best results We continuously innovate and collaborate with bright minds to push our limits and go beyond of what is possible. Together as a team we embrace the challenges and are united by our unwavering dedication towards sustainable engineering.
