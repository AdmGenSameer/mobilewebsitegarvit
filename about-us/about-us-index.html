TGR 1.0

• The first edition of Team GarVIT was established in 2018 at VIT Bhopal University.
• Despite being a new team without any past experiences, the team was able to fabricate an electric ATV which lasted the entire endurance run of ATVC with zero breakdown. TGR 1.0 secured overall AIR 6 in ATVC 2020 and completed the endurance run at third position.
• TGR 1.0, led by bright minds, created an incredible legacy through perseverance and ingenuity.

TGR 2.0

• Carrying the legacy of TGR 1.0, TGR 2.0 ascended to new heights fueled by unwavering passion and commitment to innovations.
• Driven by dedication and tenacity and the invaluable mentorship and guidance from TGR 1.0, TGR 2.0 achieved All India Rank 5 with AIR 2 in render reveal challenge and AIR 3 in manufacturing presentation.
• Apart from these feats, TGR 2.0 were able to secure top 10 AIR in various events and tests such as the cost event, design event and brake tests.
• By setting new benchmarks of excellence with their achievements, TGR 2.0 have raised the bar, urging the subsequent teams to strive for even greater heights.

TGR 3.0

• Infused with an insatiable passion for engineering marvels, TGR 3.0 embarked on a journey to create 'Vidyuth', an extraordinary ATV that epitomizes their relentless pursuit of excellence. With every intricate detail meticulously crafted, Vidyuth emerged as a beacon of their unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of design innovation.
• Building on the legacy of TGR 2.0, TGR 3.0 soared to new heights, securing international rank of 15 in overall static events with IR 3, IR 4 and IR 6 in manufacturing, sales report, and design evaluation respectively.
• As TGR 3.0 concludes its remarkable journey, their legacy of innovation and excellence continues to inspire and guide future teams.
